About Us

Welcome to ORBIX! We are dedicated to providing a seamless and efficient delivery management system for delivery personnel. Our platform helps track deliveries, monitor income, and manage weekly plans effectively.

Our mission is to streamline the delivery process and provide delivery professionals with the tools they need to stay organized and maximize earnings. With real-time updates and a user-friendly dashboard, ORBIX makes delivery tracking simple and professional.

Thank you for being part of ORBIX. We are committed to supporting your success every step of the way!
