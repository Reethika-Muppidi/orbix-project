Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I track my deliveries?

A: Log in to your ORBIX account and check your dashboard to see all assigned deliveries and their status.

Q2: How is my weekly premium calculated?

A: Weekly premium is fixed per week and may vary depending on completed deliveries. It is shown on your dashboard under “Income Details”.

Q3: Can I update my personal information?

A: Currently, personal information can be updated by contacting ORBIX support. Future updates may allow in-app editing.

Q4: Who do I contact for support?

A: For any issues, please email support@orbix.com or call our helpline.
